Why go solar?

With the cost of solar dropping to record levels and the new federal incentives, the real question is why not?

There are various reasons to add a solar to your home or business. For some people the main reason is reducing their carbon footprint. For other others the investment is too good to miss. There is also the need to gain independence and protection from the power electric grid…

Texans are taking control of their electricity cost

Producing power at your home or business reduces the dependency on power from the retail electric provider, which translates into less exposure to seasonal changes, rate hikes and surprise fees. Whether using a solar loan or a cash purchase, your cost is fixed for the next 30+ years.
